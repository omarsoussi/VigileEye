================================================================================
                    VIGILEYE — PRODUCT BACKLOG SUMMARY
================================================================================

Project: VigileEye Security Camera Management System
Architecture: Microservices (Clean Architecture)
Total Sprints: 4 | Sprint Duration: 2 weeks each

================================================================================
                           PRODUCT BACKLOG OVERVIEW
================================================================================

SPRINT 1: CAMERA MANAGEMENT BACKEND
    Build the core Camera Management microservice for managing camera lifecycle,
    configuration, status monitoring, health tracking, and basic access control.

SPRINT 2: AUTHENTICATION BACKEND
    Develop secure user authentication with 2FA, email verification, password
    reset, Google OAuth integration, and JWT token management.

SPRINT 3: MEMBERS INVITATION BACKEND
    Build camera sharing system with email-based invitations, approval codes,
    permission levels (READER/EDITOR), and membership access control.

SPRINT 4: VIDEO STREAMING BACKEND
    Build real-time video streaming with WebSocket delivery, FFmpeg processing,
    multi-protocol support (HLS/RTSP/YouTube), and frame broadcasting.

================================================================================
